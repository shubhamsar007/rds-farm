Our Story

Built on Warmth,

RD Developers began with a single belief — that every guest deserves to feel at home, whether they're staying for a night or celebrating the most important day of their lives.

From our 50-room hotel in the heart of Ahmedabad to the sprawling greenery of RDS Farm and RDS Farm 2, we've spent years quietly building something special: spaces where memories are made, not manufactured.
